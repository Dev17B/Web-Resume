In the Winter of 2024, Ontario Tech University enlisted the expertise of our team to design and implement a comprehensive LAN and WAN infrastructure solution. With campuses in north Oshawa, Toronto, and Ottawa, the university sought to interconnect these locations while ensuring robust Internet access and redundancy. Our task involved configuring MPLS WAN connections between Toronto and Ottawa, along with a DMVPN backup utilizing Internet connectivity. Additionally, we were tasked with designing and implementing a new switched LAN infrastructure for the Toronto campus. Through meticulous planning and execution, we addressed each aspect of the project, from IP addressing to routing protocol configuration. Our solution incorporated logical and physical topology diagrams, ensuring seamless connectivity and optimal performance across all campuses. Thorough testing validated the integrity and functionality of our network, demonstrating our commitment to delivering tailored solutions that meet Ontario Tech's exact specifications and future needs.
